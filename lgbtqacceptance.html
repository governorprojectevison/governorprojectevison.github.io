Our Solution

We can’t make people accept others but we can create more facilities and safe places in California where people are open and willing to do so.                                                                                                                                                                                                                 

This is the road to making California a state where everyone can feel like they belong here. 

                                                                                                                                                                                        -Nora Evison (2017)

Modeled off the outstanding Berkeley facility managed by the Pacific Center for Human Growth, we will be able to build more places where the LGBTQ community can connect with groups and have meetings with others who can relate to their stories, where people who face any type of identity issues can find refuge and acceptance.
